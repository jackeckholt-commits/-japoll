About the Project

A simple approach to comparing and anyaliseing polling avreages.

Purpose

This site acts as an aggregator of aggregators. The idea being that an avrage of avreages is more accurate Instead of relying on one polling average, it compares several polling averages to get a broader view of the political environment while also adding personal adjustments from my presonal algorithim.

Sources

The tracker compares national averages from sources such as VoteHub, Silver Bulletin, The New York Times, CNN, Race to WH, and FiftyPlusOne.

Average of averages

The current avreage takes each source equal weight. Sources without an available average are listed, but they are not included in the calculation until a usable value is available.

Current calculations

The generic ballot average currently uses VoteHub, Silver Bulletin, CNN, Race to WH, and FiftyPlusOne. The New York Times is unavailable for the generic ballot because thes no usable national generic ballot average is available. The generic ballot is also modified my my algorithim in an atempt to better account for polling errors.

The Trump approval average currently uses The New York Times, Silver Bulletin, CNN, VoteHub, Race to WH, and FiftyPlusOne.

Algorithm adjustment

The generic ballot page includes an optional adjustment based on my algorithm. The main reason for this is that for the past elections polling has been off but ive added an ajustment that comes form my math which sould make the final results more accurate.
